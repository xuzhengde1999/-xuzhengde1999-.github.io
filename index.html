COmputational Spintronics and Magnetism andLab

I joined School of Information Science and Technology in ShanghaiTech University as an Assistant Professor. I obtained my PhD degree in Engineering from National University of Singapore in Sept. 2019. I obtained my Bachelor degree in Microelectronics from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in July 2014

Our current research focus on the theoretical and numerical study (e.g., macrospin, micromagnetic and atomistic modelling) of antiferromagnets and ferrimagnets based spintronic devices, including the MRAM, spintronic oscillator, in memory computing and bio-inspired computing. I am currenly a reviewer for J. Appl. Phys., Jpn. J. Appl. Phys. and Appl. Phys. A

祝智峰于2014年获电子科技大学电子科学与技术（微电子技术）学士学位，2019年获新加坡国立大学电子工程博士学位。他博士毕业后在新加坡国立大学电子工程系进行博士后研究。他于2020年1月全职加入上海科技大学信息科学与技术学院担任助理教授、研究员、博士生导师。他主要从事自旋电子器件和电路的理论和计算研究，包括磁随机存储器、自旋电子振荡器、类脑存算一体化器件和类脑计算器件等。目前主持上海市扬帆计划项目(2020-2023)，在Nature Electronics、 Nature Nanotechnology、 Physical Review B、Physical Review Applied、 IEEE VLSI、IEEE Electron Devices Letters等顶级期刊和会议上发表文章，并担任Journal of Applied Physics、Japanese Journal of Applied Physics、Applied Physics A 审稿人。

电力系统保护与自动化实验室现招收2022级硕士/博士研究生。学生需要有较强的学习/科研热情，并拥有电气工程及其自动化、数学、电子工程、自动化或控制等相关专业背景。本实验室将优先考虑数学基础较好、逻辑思维能力较强的学生。申请者请将你的简历、个人成绩单以及个人陈述发送给刘宇博士邮箱：liuyu@shanghaitech.edu.cn。

Post-Doc Researchers Openings

PSPAL is seeking Post-Doc Researchers with strong interests in power and energy areas including but not limited to power system/microgrid protection, fault locating, dynamic/static state estimation, control and optimization. A Ph.D. degree in electric power engineering, power system, power electronics, control or related areas is required. After finishing Post-Doc research, top Post-Docs have the opportunity to join ShanghaiTech as research assistant professors .

电力系统保护与自动化实验室长期招聘博士后研究人员。聘用人员将从事电力系统/微网的保护、故障定位、控制与优化等领域的前沿研究。申请者应拥有或即将获得电气工程、电力系统、电力电子、控制或相关专业博士学位；拥有电力系统/微电网保护、故障定位、控制或优化等相关经验者优先。优秀人员可在博后出站后留校工作（助理研究员，正式编制）。
